About Me

WHO
I AM

I'm a freshly graduated high school student with a deep passion for art powered by technology. From designing school publications to crafting 3D animations, I've spent my academic years building a creative skill set that blends aesthetics with technical precision.

As the Layout Artist of my school and Technical Director in the TV Broadcasting & Journalism organization, I've led visual production for events, publications, and broadcasts — always pushing the quality bar higher.

I take commissions for layout work and video graphics, working closely with colleagues and seniors to bring their visions to life. I'm flexible, fast, and fully committed to delivering exactly what my clients need.
